Our Survey Process

Surveying isn't just sticking stakes in the ground. It's a disciplined profession that involves legal research, precision fieldwork, and licensed drafting. Here's how every project unfolds.

⏱️

Typical Timelines

  • Boundary Survey: 2–4 weeks
  • Topographic Survey: 1–3 weeks
  • ALTA/NSPS: 3–6 weeks
  • Construction Staking: 3–5 days

Timelines depend on records availability, project size, and scheduling. Rush service is available on select project types.

📋

What You'll Receive

  • Signed & sealed plat/map (PDF + CAD)
  • Corner monuments set per NMAC
  • Deed description (if requested)
  • Digital survey file for your records
📁

Help Us Help You

  • Prior surveys or plats
  • Deed or title commitment
  • Closing documents
  • Any neighbor agreements

Providing existing documents reduces research time and project cost.
